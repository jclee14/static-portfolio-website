Harry Potter and Elder

           It still sustains in this world, the Elder Wand. To prevent it from dark wizards, the chosen boy have to protect it with his friends. The true last war is beginning.
           “Harry Potter” was a film that was created in visual effects class while I was studying in college. In the beginning, my team and I decided to make an important mistake, because we wrote the story that was not powerful and much of techniques. After a half time before submitting day, we made a valuable decision by changing the story into the new one, and Harry Potter chronicle was an adapted concept which my group willingly selected. The imagination was exploded out in this short moment. Our artists enjoyed working as much as we played. Anyway, I would like to say thank you to our fault that led us to meet this destination.
