God President

           What will be happened in Thailand, if our country’s supreme leader act a corrupt behavior? Some bad desire becomes the evil effects as similar to God’s power which impacts to a huge area.
           “God President” was the very first 3D animation which I created with my friends for the competition. It was one of my favorite work because I could play with my imagination and design. I designed the scenes by placing the 3D characters on the real video footage, thus it magnified the core message to the viewer visually. We knew nothing at that time, hence we did our best. For example, I animated all characters by using the vertex point! This was really crazy, however, we reached our goal, not about winning the award, but we could create 3D animation by ourselves.
