FAKEBOOK

           For your information, every day, there are 1-3 people who lost from their house in Thailand. This terrifying news was found when my team was researching the story for making a contest animation for kids. The topic was “Thai Kid and IT”. The competitor must deliver any kind of media which contained the content about kid and technology issue in our society, hence we selected kid losing topic for our project because the mentioned problem was closer than we thought. In conclusion, “Fakebook” was our second 3D animation project.
           Because there was a short time before the deadline, we solved this challenge by telling the story within one scene and still camera. These solutions enlarged the content effectively and brought us to the second place on this stage.
