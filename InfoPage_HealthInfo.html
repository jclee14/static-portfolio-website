Healthcare Infographic

           When I did a freelance job with my friends, we got an opportunity from a government organization to create a 2D infographic for educating Thai and Myanmar people in my city about the information of diseases in a kid. This was why we picked the easy-watching visual to be the main subject and used it to drive all the components in this project. The graphic linked the relationship between kid and parent and convinced an audience to follow the instructions by parent instinct. In addition, the processes were not complex as we thought first, but the challenge was the 3 episodes of animation with limited working time. At last, we could deliver this project to the client on the appropriate time.
