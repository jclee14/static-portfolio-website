Dental Music Video

           Back to your childhood, had you ever got caries in your teeth? I assure that most of the kids in Thailand facing this huge health problem, because of them, especially their parents, lack of dental knowledge.
           This is the reason why faculty of dentistry, Prince of Songkla University, produced these useful and cute 2D animations for educating Thai people. Fortunately, my team participated in this project as a freelance artist. Our job was to create music videos from three provided songs, hence we chose 2D animation technique for this project, because it suited to our target, 3-10 years kids. Our team wrote stories and animated lovely characters supported by one of my friend, and other elements. As a result, there were lots of feedback from both kids and parents that some of them could sing along the videos, and follow the dentistâ€™s suggestion. This project is one of my favorite animation in my experience.
